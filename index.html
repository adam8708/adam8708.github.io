Kalidou Koulibaly

Clapton Orient FC are proud to announce that Kalidou Koulibaly has been voted as the inaugural Player of the Month.

Joining at the start of a journey that has taken us from the lowly depths of Division 10, to the dizzy heights of The top flight, Kalidou has shown great grit and determination to ensure that the success of Clapton Orient is not just temporary. He has been instrumental in all three division 1 titles and a key component in a cup competition that saw us reach the semi-finals. He is a player that leads by example and, as the votes show, has earned the respect of the dressing room.

Boasting a 75% tackle success is no easy feat in Division 1 but Kalidou is in a class of his own. Striking fear in to the opponents attacking players, he continues to assert his dominance as a central defender by brushing aside highly decorated strikers.

It is no surprise that Kalidou has picked up the first Player of the Month award and it will surely not be his last.

Congratulations Kalidou Koulibaly!
